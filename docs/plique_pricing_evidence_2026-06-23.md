## Why the anchor was rewritten (2026-06-23)

**Prior anchor** (deployed earlier 2026-06-23, removed same day):
> "Plique-à-jour at high-jewelry maisons typically starts at **$5,000+** · This comes directly from the bench, no maison markup."

**Risk**: vague "$5,000+" was a market estimate, not a citable benchmark. Under CN 广告法 §24, comparative claims need verifiable evidence; a buyer producing a $400 Etsy plique listing labeled "Lalique-style" could argue the anchor is misleading.

**New anchor** uses real hammer prices from three named auction houses, with year + lot URL traceable to each. Defensible under both CN 广告法 and FTC §5 (deceptive practices).
